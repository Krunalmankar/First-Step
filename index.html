Engineering Excellence in ServiceNow Architecture

A structured documentation platform covering enterprise-grade ITOM, CMDB, CSDM, and integration architecture patterns.

Platform Vision

Excellency is designed as a long-term architecture knowledge system. It documents real-world implementation strategies, governance frameworks, and scalable design patterns across complex ServiceNow environments.

Core Domains

IT Operations Management (ITOM): Discovery orchestration, MID Server architecture, Service Mapping topology, and Event Management correlation strategies.

Configuration Management Database (CMDB): CI modeling standards, reconciliation governance, relationship integrity, and lifecycle management.

Common Service Data Model (CSDM): Business service alignment, application services, ownership structure, and architectural layering.

Purpose

The goal is not content volume, but architectural clarity. Each article focuses on design decisions, trade-offs, scalability considerations, and governance impact.
