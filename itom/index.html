ServiceNow IT Operations Management (ITOM) Overview

Enterprise-grade visibility, service health monitoring, and AI-driven operational automation.

What is ServiceNow ITOM?

ServiceNow IT Operations Management (ITOM) is a comprehensive suite that enhances IT visibility, service health, and operational efficiency through intelligent automation. It enables organizations to shift from reactive firefighting to proactive service assurance by combining infrastructure discovery, service dependency mapping, event correlation, and AI-driven analytics.

Core Components and Functional Capabilities

Discovery

Discovery creates a comprehensive and continuously updated inventory of IT infrastructure across cloud and on-premises environments. It identifies servers, network devices, applications, databases, and cloud resources, populating the CMDB with authoritative configuration data.

Service Mapping

Service Mapping visualizes relationships between business services and their underlying infrastructure components. It enables impact analysis by mapping dependencies between applications, servers, databases, and network layers.

Event Management

Event Management ingests events from monitoring tools and infrastructure sources. Using correlation rules and AI-driven logic, it filters noise and identifies actionable alerts, reducing alert fatigue and improving response precision.

Cloud Provisioning & Governance

ITOM supports automation and governance of cloud environments, enabling cost optimization, lifecycle management, and standardized provisioning across multi-cloud architectures.

Operational Intelligence / AIOps

Through machine learning and advanced analytics, ITOM analyzes log data, metrics, and performance trends to detect anomalies and predict potential service degradation.

Certificate Management

ITOM discovers and manages TLS/SSL certificates across the infrastructure, preventing outages caused by certificate expiration and ensuring compliance.

Architectural Benefits

Increased Visibility

ITOM provides a unified, end-to-end view of the IT landscape, eliminating blind spots across hybrid infrastructure environments.

Reduced MTTR

By correlating events and mapping service dependencies, ITOM enables rapid root cause identification and accelerates resolution time.

Improved Productivity

Automation reduces manual operational tasks, allowing engineering teams to focus on strategic initiatives.

Proactive Operations

ITOM shifts operations from a reactive break-fix model to predictive, prevention-based service management.

Integration with ITSM

ITOM integrates directly with ServiceNow IT Service Management (ITSM), transforming infrastructure events into actionable incidents. This ensures alignment between operational monitoring and service delivery workflows, improving overall service reliability and customer satisfaction.
